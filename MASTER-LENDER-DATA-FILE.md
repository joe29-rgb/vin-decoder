## SUMMARY: KEY DIFFERENCES

### PRIME LENDERS:
- Lower rates (0%-10.99%)
- Stricter credit (FICO 650+)
- Lower LTV (90%-120%)
- Terms up to 96 months
- Bank-backed financing

### SUBPRIME LENDERS:
- Higher rates (11.49%-32%)
- Accept all credit profiles
- Higher LTV (110%-180%)
- Terms up to 84-96 months
- Higher dealer reserves
- GPS/SID devices often required
- Non-bank finance companies
