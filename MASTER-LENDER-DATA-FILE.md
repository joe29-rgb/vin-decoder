## KEY DIFFERENCES: PRIME VS SUBPRIME

### Prime Lenders Characteristics:
- Lower rates (0%-15%)
- Stricter credit requirements (FICO 620+)
- Lower LTV ratios (90%-140%)
- Longer terms available (up to 96 months)
- Lower dealer reserves
- Focus on creditworthy customers

### Subprime Lenders Characteristics:
- Higher rates (11.99%-32%)
- Accept all credit profiles (FICO 300+)
- Higher LTV ratios (110%-180%)
- Flexible terms (up to 84 months)
- Higher dealer reserves
- GPS/SID devices often required
- Focus on credit-challenged customers
