## How It Works

When you enter an approval (e.g., "TD 4-Key with $900 payment"):

1. **System looks up lender program:** TD 4-Key
2. **Retrieves correct rate:** 17.5%
3. **Retrieves max term:** 84 months
4. **Calculates max advance:** Based on $900 payment, 17.5% rate, 84 months
5. **Scores all inventory:** Using these correct parameters
6. **Maximizes profit:** By using longest allowable term and highest advance

**Result:** Every vehicle gets the maximum amortization allowed by that specific lender program, ensuring you maximize deal size and profit potential.
